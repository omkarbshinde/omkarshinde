call centre performance

In recent years, call centers have evolved beyond traditional voice-based interactions

Introduction

This dashboard tracks the performance of agents, customer satisfaction, and average speed of answer in a call center. The data is collected from a variety of sources, including call center software, customer surveys, and agent performance reviews.

Technologies used

Excel and Power BI

Data

The following data is tracked on the dashboard:
- Agent performance: This data tracks the number of calls answered, the average call time, and the number of customer complaints.
- Customer satisfaction: This data tracks the customer satisfaction with the call center, the quality of the service, and the timeliness of the response.
- Average speed of answer: This data tracks the average time it takes for an agent to answer a call.

analysis


The data on the dashboard is analyzed to identify trends and areas for improvement. For example, if the average speed of answer is increasing, it may indicate that the call center is understaffed. If the customer satisfaction score is decreasing, it may indicate that the call center needs to improve its training program.

Conclusion

The dashboard provides call center managers with a valuable tool to track performance, identify trends, and make informed decisions about how to improve the call center.
